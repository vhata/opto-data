--- title: How to spell "Jonathan" ---
  1. Start with a capital "J".
  2. After the first "o" comes an "n", not an "h".
  3. The "n" is followed by an "a", not an "o".
  4. After the "th" comes another "a" - not an "o".

Voila: Jonathan
